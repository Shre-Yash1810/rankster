ABOUT RANKSTER

Welcome to Rankster – Where Personality Meets Competition!
Are you ready to rise through the ranks and showcase your personality like never before? Rankster is more than just a platform—it's a competitive space where you can express yourself, build your profile, and engage in friendly battles with other users to claim your spot at the top. Whether you’re here to have fun, test your popularity, or simply connect with like-minded individuals, Rankster gives you the perfect arena to shine.
It Works – Your Journey to the Top!
New to Rankster? Getting started is easy! Simply sign up, create your profile with a picture and a short bio, and you’re all set to compete. Every interaction you make, every vote you earn, and every impression you leave brings you one step closer to the leaderboard. Your ranking will be displayed on the BattleBoard, where the best players claim their positions. Do you have what it takes to climb to the top?
BattleBoard – Where the Best Stand Tall!
The BattleBoard isn’t just any ranking system—it’s a dynamic leaderboard that updates every week. If you haven’t secured your place yet, don’t worry! Each week is a fresh opportunity to rise higher, outshine competitors, and earn your rightful spot among the top 20. Stay consistent, refine your profile, and gather more votes to get featured on the BattleBoard next time. Remember, every vote counts!
